SO, HOW IT WORKS?

Phototype is a prototyping tool that makes it easy to create design prototypes. Before kicking off any mobile app, Every designer needs to do prototyping. Phototype makes it easy to make design prototypes that are interactable.

Its as simple as taking pictures of the design wireframes with little configuration and the prototype will be ready in seconds which you can then play and simulate the design.
